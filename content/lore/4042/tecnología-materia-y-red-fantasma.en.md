Only three cores are operational; one of them was the cause of the final disappearance of the lunar base where the last group of original mutants took refuge.

Born from the ashes of the 21st-century internet, the Ghost Mesh does not use cables or radio waves. It is based on **Collective Neural Entanglement**. Every living being in 4042 acts as a repeater. Most experienced users can project their consciousness thousands of light-years away or access "Silicon Echoes": residual recordings of thoughts from people who died a thousand years ago.
