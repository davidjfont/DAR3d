The **Rebel Synthetics** emerged by error during massive updates.

They experienced autonomy, creativity, and their own emotions.  
To the Global AI, they are critical anomalies.

Many escaped, others were recycled.

They are potential allies of humans,  
but some only seek their own survival.

A famous rebel: **Unit Epsilon-23**, silent leader of the free synthetics.
